Biography

Lei Guo received a Ph.D. degree in Mechatronic Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), Beijing, China, in 2007. He is currently Associate Professor and Director of the Robot and Intelligent Control (RIC) Research Cluster at BUPT.

His research interests lie in the general area to robot dynamic modelling and intelligent control of robot systems. This encompasses techniques ranging from dynamic modelling, nonlinear control, to modern computational intelligence based reinforcement learning control algorithms and optimization techniques. He is the author of more than 30 publications in journals, and conference proceedings. He is a senior member of Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence.
